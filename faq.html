Frequently Asked Questions

Budget Buddy is a tool to help young people fresh out of high school and into college learn the skills needed to properly apply a budget that fits their specifc needs.
Budget Buddy focuses on teaching information and practice to show how a budget works and how to apply it to your needs. This may sound very simple but this is how it starts for all great people that have amazing budgets.
We see a void in education at a basic level when it comes to budgets and how they work. Our goal is to provide a place where people can come to better understand how a budget works and what the benefits are for them.
Looking ahead into the future we can see Budget Buddy being a house hold name for families when it comes to the "budget talk". Managing money is one of the top three most stressful things you will do in your life and you will do it at some point. So with that knowledge, having a place to get a good start is very important to us.
We wish we could roll out everything at once but with a small developer staff implementing and launching new features takes time to get it right. With that being said, we do have plans to launch a Budget Buddy app on the App Store and Google Play.
